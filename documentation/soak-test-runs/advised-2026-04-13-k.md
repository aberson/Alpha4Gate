## Findings

1. **Curriculum escalation works.** Training at diff 3 (run 2159) was not sufficient for diff 5 transfer. Training at diff 4 in this run produced a policy that wins at diff 5 at least some of the time.
2. **Short, focused soaks are viable.** 4 rollouts + 4 eval games (~8 min total) generated enough training signal to move the needle, provided you stop before the full 20-game eval batch locks the daemon.
3. **Game crashes happen** (1/3 this validation). Known `ConnectionAlreadyClosedError` — suggests a retry/resume mechanism would be useful for future runs.
4. **Reward rules from prior runs** (supply-cap-12/15min, attack-state boosts from runs 2110 and 2145) are now baked into a checkpoint that demonstrably wins at the target difficulty. The full stack worked together.
