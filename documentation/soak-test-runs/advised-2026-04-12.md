## Key Finding

The bot's biggest problem is **rule-based passivity**, not training. Games regularly stall at 200/200 supply with 5-10k minerals floating because the rule-based strategy doesn't force decisive attacks or efficient spending at max supply. Reward shaping improvements will only manifest when PPO is trained and used (--decision-mode neural or hybrid).
