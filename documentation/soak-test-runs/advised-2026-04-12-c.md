## Iteration 3

- **Selected improvement:** Stronger worker excess penalties (rank #1, type: training)
- **Principle(s) addressed:** §4.1 (Worker Production), §5.2 (Production Scaling), §24 (Common Failure Modes)
- **What changed:** `excess-workers-50` from -0.004 to -0.015 (3.75x), `excess-workers-65` from -0.008 to -0.03 (3.75x)
- **Analysis findings:** Bot consistently produces 60-72 probes in every game. With supply capped at 84-100, workers consume 70-85% of supply leaving barely any room for army. This is the root cause of the stalling — the bot has a massive economy but no army to spend it on.
- **Validation result:** PASS (2/2 wins, no regression)
- **Metrics:** 2/2 quick wins, consistent with prior iterations
- **Wall clock used:** ~75m of 4h remaining
